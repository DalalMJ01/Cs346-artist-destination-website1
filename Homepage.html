Pottery

In a pottery class, participants delve into the art of shaping clay into functional or decorative objects through hand-building techniques or on a pottery wheel. Guided by an experienced instructor, students learn fundamental skills such as wedging, molding, trimming, and glazing, fostering both creativity and craftsmanship in a hands-on, collaborative environment.

Crochet

A crochet class offers hands-on instruction in the art of crocheting, teaching students how to create beautiful fabric using yarn and hooks. Participants learn a variety of stitches and techniques, from beginner basics to advanced patterns, in a supportive environment conducive to creativity and skill development.
